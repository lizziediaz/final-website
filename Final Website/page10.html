ROCKY ROAD ICE CREAM

If you're looking for an ice cream flavor that isn't as plain as regular chocolate ice cream, then Rocky Road is the ice cream for you.

Rocky Road ice cream usually has nuts and marshmallows mixed in, so there is no need to add any toppings
